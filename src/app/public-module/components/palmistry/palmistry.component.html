The art or practice of supposedly interpreting a person's character or predicting their future by examining the lines and other features of the hand, especially the palm and fingers. (Dictionary.com)

There are two parts in palmistry cheirognomy refers to the pads and mounts of the palm and judging the positive and negative traits in a person's philosophy. Cheriomancy relates to the marks and lines of the palm in analyzing the future events of the person (askatrologer.com)
