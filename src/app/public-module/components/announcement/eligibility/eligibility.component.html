Eligibility

Eligibility requirements to be selected and participate in “Indians and Religion in the US’ (“Program”), applicants must meet the following requirements:

  • All applicants must be at least twenty-one (21) years of age at the time of application.
  • All applicants must be versed in Hindu religious/spiritual customs and knowledge.
  • All applicants must be residing in the USA/Canada.
  • Applicants may not presently be a candidate for any type of elected US/Canadian political office ("Candidate").
  • All applicants must have a passport/green card/H1B Visa valid for one (1) year following the submission of the application.
  • All applicants must be in the field for at least 2 years or more.
  • Applicants must never have been convicted of a felony or ever had a restraining order entered against them.
