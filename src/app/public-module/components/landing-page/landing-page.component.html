How it Works

Find a Pandit

Find a Pandit sitting at the comfort of your home. Enjoy a hastle-free experience finding the right pandit for your next occasion. Simply enter your zip code and find a pandit for your next wedding, housewarming, child naming ceremony, etc.

View Pandit Profile

Upon clicking search, you will be presented with a list of Pandits in your local area. You can click on the Pandits profile, review, and select the one that suits your needs.

Schedule Your Puja

Once you find a pandit you like, simply click on “Schedule a Puja”, enter all the necessary information and click submit. The pandit will be notified of your request and reach out to you.
