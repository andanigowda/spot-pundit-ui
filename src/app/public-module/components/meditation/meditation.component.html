Meditating is thinking deeply or focusing one's mind for a period, in silence or with the aid of chanting, for religious or spiritual purposes, or as a relaxation method. In Hindu Sanskriti mainly there are three stages of meditation: dharana (concentration), dhyanam (meditation) and samadhim (Enlightenment.)

The most popular types of meditation include Vipassana (Mindfulness) and TM (Transcendental), but there are infinitely more meditations than these two.
